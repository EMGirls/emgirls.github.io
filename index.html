EMGirls is a youth-led nonprofit organization that aims to empower girls by continually challenging them to step out of their comfort zones.


What is EMGirls?

EMGirls strives to empower young women interested in entrepreneurship -- we strive to bridge the gap of gender disparity in the entrepreneurial field by providing meaningful resources and connections within the community. Founded in April 2020, our primary focus has been to help young girls unearth their entrepreneurial spirit, reach their full potential, and ultimately, shape a new generation of young women.
