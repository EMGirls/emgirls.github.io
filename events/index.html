EVENTS

EMGirls is a youth-led non-profit organization that aims to empower women in fields where there are evident gender gaps, such as with entrepreneurship. We aim to challenge girls to step outside their comfort zones and discover their interests and motives to create a change!

On this page, you can find information on the current events being hosted, and any additional events that our team is planning for the foreseeable future.

EMGirls Entrepreneurial Competition

July 2020-present

EMGirls’ Entrepreneurial Competition is our annual flagship event which was hosted in August 2020 and January 2021. We initiated this event to help transform girls’ nascent business ideas into actual businesses and/or develop their existing businesses further. Over 480+ female youth from 49 countries participated in this competition.

2P Program

May 2020

The 2P Program is a series of short lessons that expose girls to the world of entrepreneurship. Through this program, we hope to inspire and encourage girls to reach their full potential, all while providing opportunities for girls to develop their confidence, public speaking skills, and entrepreneurial knowledge.
