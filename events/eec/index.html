FAQ


What is the competition criteria?

Anyone who is female is welcome to join!

Do I need a team to compete?

You can enter EEC individually or in a team (no upper limit to the number of members). If you are looking for a team, just indicate it on the sign-up Google Form.

Do I need to have an established business to enter?

You do not need to have an established business for EEC. If you have a business idea, you can enter. Even if you do not have an idea yet, EMGirls will help you develop one during the process!
