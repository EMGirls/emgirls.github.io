EMGirls is made possible by our partners

EMGirls would like to thank our generous sponsors and supporting partners who share our mission of supporting aspiring female entrepreneurs in entrepreneurship. These alliance partnerships make it possible for EMGirls to serve youth worldwide. If you are interested in supporting EMGirls, please fill out the form below.

EMPOWER

EMGirls would like to thank McMaster University’s DeGroote School of Business, for their generous support this year. The DeGroote School of Business us based in Hamilton, Ontario and is known for being one of the top business schools in Canada. Please visit their website for more information. Thank you McMaster!

EMGirls would also like to thank the Ryerson University’s Diversity Institute partnered with the Women Entrepreneurship Knowledge Hub for their kind support this year. The WEKH are supporting female entrepreneurs by providing resources and events. Keep updated with the WEKH here here Thank you WEKH!

IGNITE

Innovation Boost Zone (IBZ) is a user-centric technology incubator at Ryerson University that supports entrepreneurs throughout their journey while providing startups with access to programs, advisors, mentorship, co-working space as well as funding. Thank you Innovation Boost Zone for sponsoring EMGirls!
