INTERN TEAM

What does it mean to be an EMintern?

Interns devise mini-projects and events throughout the year: they may solicit sponsors, network with professionals create event documents, hold webinars, etc. Your hours of hard work and dedication will be recorded. Volunteer hours and reference letters will be given upon request.
